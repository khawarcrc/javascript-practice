Factorialize a Number

function 1 explanation

Initialization:
Initialize a variable product to 1. This variable will store the cumulative product of numbers from 2 to num.
Loop Start:
Start a for loop with i initialized to 2 and continue until i is less than or equal to num.
Loop Iterations:
- Iteration 1:
i is 2, so calculate product as 1 * 2 = 2.
Console: Prints 2
- Iteration 2:
i is 3, so calculate product as 2 * 3 = 6.
Console: Prints 6
- Iteration 3:
i is 4, so calculate product as 6 * 4 = 24.
Console: Prints 24
- Iteration 4:
i is 5, so calculate product as 24 * 5 = 120.
Console: Prints 120
Loop End:
The loop completes when i becomes 6, which is greater than num (5).
Return:
The final result, product, is 120, which is the factorial of 5. This value is returned by the function.
